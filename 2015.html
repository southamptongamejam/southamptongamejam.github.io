Southampton Game Jam

January 23 - 25, 2015

Global Game Jam 2015 is now over. Thank you very much to our volunteers for ensuring the success of the Southampton site, to our sponsors for allowing us to provide food and prizes, and to all of our participants for producing such great games. We hope you all enjoyed it. Check back soon for information on our next game jam.

About

Southampton Game Jam is a local site for the Global Game Jam 2015, which took place on the weekend of 23rd-25th January 2015. Our site at the University of Southampton Highfield Campus had 24 games created by 92 participants.
